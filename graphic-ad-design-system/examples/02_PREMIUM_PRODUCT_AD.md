# Example 2 — Premium Matcha Product Advertisement

ตัวอย่างนี้แสดงวิธีเรียนรู้ Luxury Editorial DNA โดยไม่คัดลอกฉากสีดำ–เขียวหรือการวางกระป๋องจาก Reference

## Brief

- Brand: Hikari Tea House (สมมติ)
- Product: Single-origin ceremonial matcha
- Objective: สร้างภาพลักษณ์งานคราฟต์ร่วมสมัยและเพิ่มการทดลองซื้อ
- Headline: Quiet Energy
- Offer: Complimentary tasting spoon with first order
- Output: 4:5 social ad

## Reference Transfer

นำมาใช้:

- Typography ที่สงบและมีพื้นที่หายใจ
- Product เป็นวัตถุมีคุณค่า
- Palette จำกัด
- Material และแสงสร้าง Premium perception
- Information hierarchy ชัด

ไม่คัดลอก:

- พื้นหลังดำ
- ระนาบสีเขียวเฉียง
- Bowl matcha ด้านหน้า
- Product arrangement เดิม
- Headline และ Offer layout เดิม

## Selected Concept

“Quiet Energy” ถูกถ่ายทอดผ่านวงแสงยามเช้าที่เคลื่อนผ่านพื้นกระดาษวาชิและหยุดบนกระป๋องชาเพียงหนึ่งใบ ส่วนผงมัทฉะปรากฏเป็นเส้นบางที่วาดวงโคจรรอบสินค้า ไม่ใช้ชามชาเป็น Hero

## Final Generation Prompt

```text
INTENDED USE
Create a vertical 4:5 premium social advertisement for Hikari Tea House single-origin ceremonial matcha.

CORE MESSAGE
Ceremonial matcha provides a calm, deliberate form of daily energy.

CREATIVE CONCEPT
Visualize “Quiet Energy” through a controlled circle of warm morning light crossing a tactile washi-paper surface and stopping precisely on one hero matcha tin. A fine trail of matcha powder forms a restrained partial orbit around the tin, suggesting energy without using arrows, lightning, motion graphics, or athletic imagery.

DESIGN MODE
Contemporary Japanese-inspired editorial product still life. Restrained, tactile, quiet, and precise. Avoid conventional black-and-gold luxury styling.

COMPOSITION
Use an asymmetric editorial grid with generous margins.

Place the hero tin slightly below and right of optical center, occupying approximately one-third of the canvas height. Reserve the upper-left for the headline. Place the offer and brand signature in a small aligned group near the lower-left.

Use one large circular patch of sunlight as a compositional anchor. The powder orbit should guide the eye toward the product without enclosing it completely.

VIEWING ORDER
1. Hero matcha tin
2. Circular morning light and powder orbit
3. “Quiet Energy” headline
4. Offer and brand

PRODUCT FIDELITY
Use the supplied Hikari Tea House product packshot as authoritative. Preserve its cylindrical proportion, cream paper label, dark-green cap, logo, Japanese characters, color, and label placement exactly. Do not recreate or reinterpret the packaging.

BACKGROUND
Use layered off-white and pale mineral-gray washi-paper surfaces with subtle fibers, deckled transitions, and low-relief geometry. The background exists to reveal texture and receive morning light. It must remain quieter than the product.

TYPOGRAPHY
Headline:
“Quiet Energy”

Use a refined high-contrast serif with calm proportions and generous letter spacing. Keep it medium-large rather than oversized.

Supporting line:
“Single-origin ceremonial matcha”

Use a small neutral sans-serif with precise tracking.

Offer:
“Complimentary tasting spoon with first order”

Keep all supporting type aligned to one editorial column. No badges, pills, or glossy CTA buttons.

COLOR SYSTEM
- dominant: warm off-white and mineral gray
- product anchor: deep tea green
- type: charcoal
- accent: natural matcha green used only in powder and one small typographic detail
- highlight: warm early-morning sunlight

LIGHTING
Use real early-morning window light from upper-right with a softly defined circular shape, subtle falloff, and a long restrained shadow toward lower-left. Add minimal fill so label detail remains legible. Maintain natural white balance and quiet contrast.

MATERIAL AND REALISM
Show paper fibers, fine powder granularity, matte printed label texture, restrained metal-cap reflection, soft dust variation, and natural surface irregularity. Keep edges crisp where focus falls and gently soften distant paper layers.

ANTI-AI CONTROLS
No floating leaves, no tea plantation background, no steam, no glowing particles, no gold ornaments, no marble pedestal, no extra tea utensils, no excessive powder explosion, and no dramatic cinematic landscape.

FINAL QUALITY
The artwork should feel like a professionally photographed and typeset editorial product campaign, with value communicated through restraint, material, light, scale, and spacing.
```
